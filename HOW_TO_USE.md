# Placify: User Training Guide (Role-Based)

This guide provides step-by-step instructions for each role within the Placify CRM. Follow your specific role section to understand your responsibilities and workflows.

---

## 1. Administrator (`administrator`) & Manager (`jpc_manager`)
**Primary Responsibility**: System oversight, user management, and high-level pipeline monitoring.

### Workflow:
1.  **User Management**: Use the **Team** page to add new members, reset passwords, or change roles.
2.  **Pipeline Monitoring**: Monitor the **Dashboard** for overall team performance and candidate status counts (Active, Completed, Not Interested).
3.  **Feature Blasts (Alerts)**:
    *   Navigate to **Feature Alerts**.
    *   Click **New Feature** to notify specific teams about CRM updates.
    *   Attach images/screenshots or PDF guides to ensure everyone understands the new "Feathers" (Features).
4.  **App Tracker Reports**: Generate XLSX reports in the App Tracker to review recruiter activity across specific dates.
5.  **Interview Coordination**:
    *   Monitor the **Interview Support** dashboard.
    *   Review **Proxy Feedback** once interviews are completed.
    *   Use the **Decision Console** to log the official company verdict (Offer/Rejected).
    *   **Privileged Action**: You have the authority to **Delete** interview requests if they are no longer valid.
6.  **Target Management**: Review and approve "Target Reduction Requests" from recruiters who cannot meet their daily application targets.

---

## 2. Customer Service (`jpc_cs`)
**Primary Responsibility**: Candidate onboarding, quality control, and recruiting team coordination.

### Workflow:
1.  **Onboarding**: Once a candidate is added, perform the **QC Call**. Use the checklist in the Candidate Detail page to ensure all requirements are met.
2.  **Agreements & Payments**: Update "Status Flags" for agreements and set up the payment plan (Part 1/2 tracking).
3.  **Recruiting Coordination**: Assign a **Recruiter** to the candidate once onboarding is complete.
4.  **Resume Management**: Review resume update requests from recruiters and forward them to the Resume Team.
5.  **Interview Support Management**:
    *   Review and approve "Interview Support Requests" submitted by recruiters.
    *   Assign **Proxies** to specific interview rounds.
    *   **Privileged Action**: You can **Delete** interviews if a candidate drops out or the role is closed.
    *   **Pipeline Management**: You have the authority to move candidates between any stage of the pipeline as needed (Admin-level stage access).
    *   **Target Management**: Review and approve "Target Reduction Requests" from recruiters who cannot meet their daily application targets.

---

## 3. Recruiter (`jpc_recruiter`)
**Primary Responsibility**: Daily job application management and interview scheduling.

### Workflow:
1.  **Daily Applications**: Use the **App Tracker** to log job applications for your assigned candidates.
2.  **Target Compliance**: Aim to hit the daily target (e.g., 40 apps). If volume is low, submit a **Target Reduction Request** via the tracker.
3.  **Resume Requests**: If a candidate needs a resume modification, submit a request in the **Resume Log Book**.
4.  **Interview Support**:
    *   When a candidate gets an interview, use the **Interview Support** dashboard to "Create Support Request".
    *   **Self Attended Mode**: If the candidate is attending personally, uncheck "Proxy Required". This allows you to "Set Schedule" directly without waiting for proxy assignment.
    *   **Job Description (JD)**: Add the JD link/text so the proxy knows the requirements (if proxy is used).
    *   Add multiple **Interview Rounds** if needed.
    *   **WhatsApp Sharing**: Use the "Share via WhatsApp" button to instantly send tailored booking links or details to the candidate.
5.  **Client Response**: Once the interview is done, monitor for the company's response and log the final result in the **Decision Console**.

---

## 4. Proxy Team (`jpc_proxy`)
**Primary Responsibility**: Technical interview support and evaluation.

### Workflow:
1.  **Availability**: Set your available slots in the **Proxy Central** dashboard. Use "Auto-Generate Slots" to quickly populate your 9:30 AM - 6:30 PM EST schedule.
2.  **Assignments**: Review upcoming interview assignments in your dashboard.
3.  **Preparation**: Click **Job Specs** or refer to the **Job Description** attached to the assignment card to prepare for the technical interview.
4.  **Feedback**: Post-interview, submit the **Technical Evaluation**. Be detailed about performance, questions asked, and any issues faced.

---

## 5. Feature Alerts System
**Stay Informed**: Whenever a new feature is launched in the CRM:
1. New alerts will appear at the top of your **Dashboard**.
2. Click the **Image** or **Documentation** links to learn more.
3. Once read, use the **X** to dismiss the alert.
4. View the full history of alerts in the **Feature Alerts** page.

---

## 6. Lead Generation (`jpc_lead_gen`) & Sales (`jpc_sales`)
**Primary Responsibility**: Bringing in new candidates and initial conversion.

### Workflow:
1.  **Adding Leads**: Use the "Add Candidate" button to enter basic info and assign to a Sales representative.
2.  **Lead Tracking**: Monitor your own leads to see when they move to the `sales` or `cs` stages.
3.  **Sales Conversion**: Sales team members contact leads and move them forward once interest is confirmed.

---

## 7. Resume Team (`jpc_resume`)
**Primary Responsibility**: Resume modification and finalization.

### Workflow:
1.  **Queue Management**: Check the **Resume Log Book** for requests assigned to you.
2.  **Processing**: Perform the requested changes and upload the final resume file.
3.  **Completion**: Mark the request as "Completed" to notify the recruiter.

---

## 8. Marketing Team (`jpc_marketing`)
**Primary Responsibility**: Profile optimization and approval flow.

### Workflow:
1.  **Approvals**: Review and approve resume change requests submitted by recruiters before they reach CS/Resume teams.
2.  **Optimization**: Coordinate LinkedIn profile updates and branding for candidates.
